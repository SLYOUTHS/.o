About SL Youth



SL Youth is dedicated to empowering St. Lucian youth by celebrating our rich cultural heritage and fostering community connections. We believe in the power of young people to create positive change and preserve our traditions for future generations.

Launched in July 2025, the SL Youth program was founded in affiliation with the Union of St. Lucia Overseas Associations. It began as an initiative to bring together young people with a shared mission: to preserve and uplift St. Lucian culture through unity, creativity, and purpose.
